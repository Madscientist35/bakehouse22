Bakehouse22 has a reputation for outstanding and delicious hospitality, great food, great coffee, and most of all great service. In addition to being located in the heart of Limerick's historic area, we pride ourselves on our warm friendly atmosphere and good quality ingredients, supporting local producers and suppliers where possible. Based in the heart of the old Limerick quarter, serving great food in a casual and friendly environment. Run by husband and wife team Mike & Sheila Tracey, the restaurant is designed to be a comforting way-post/cafe/local shop for neighbours and visitors alike.
