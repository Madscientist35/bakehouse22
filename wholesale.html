Here at Bakehause 22 we offer an extensive menu for both corporate and outside catering. We have tasty options from breakfast platters. Bakehause 22 offer an extensive menu to cater for all tastes from breakfast platters, gourmet sandwiches and hot food, & if that is not enough temptation in itself, our delicious bakery can supply cakes /desserts and breads that are baked fresh in-house by our bakers 7 days a week to ensure the freshiest of products to you. We are all about You the customer, and ensuring service and commitment to highest of quality for your occasion.

To ensure we can accommodate you to the highest standard, please order at least 48 hours in advance. To secure your booking a 50% deposit is required.

If you have any dietary requirements please let us know and we will do all to accommodate you.

Some of our products may contain the following allergens: “gluten, crustaceans, eggs, fish, peanuts, soybeans, milk, nuts, sulphites, lupin, molluscs”.

We are open 7 days a week. We are a small business in operation for over 65 years & personally involved in all aspects of catering with our staff.
