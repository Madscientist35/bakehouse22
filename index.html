Welcome To Bakehouse 22

Delicious Food & Cozy Atmosphere

BakeHouse 22, in the heart of the old Limerick quarter, serves great food in a casual and friendly environment. Run by husband and wife team Mike & Sheila Tracey, the restaurant is designed to be a comforting way-post/cafe/local shop for neighbours and visitors alike.
